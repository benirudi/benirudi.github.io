How LTS works

The Light Technology System uses active bio-optical channels—similar in concept to acupuncture meridians in Traditional Chinese Medicine, but without needles—to interact with the body’s light-information network. In injuries and fatigue, this information flow is disrupted; LTS aims to support restoration of healthy signaling so the body can do what it’s built to do: recover.

  1. 1. Targeted application along bio-optical pathways related to the affected area.
  2. 2. Gentle, non-electrical stimulation that works only with the body’s own energy.
  3. 3. Supported self-regulation: normalized signaling can help reduce fatigue and speed natural recovery.
